About Us

The Sparks Bank

The Sparks Bank Bank is a part of The Sparks Foundation. It is one of Snations's leading private banks and was among the first to receive approval from the Central bank of Snation (CBS) to set up a private sector bank in 1202 SY. Today, The Sparks Bank has a banking network of 3,019 branches and 12,028 ATM's in 2,703 cities/towns. The Sparks Bank offers a diverse range of financial products and banking services to customers(high profile businessmen) through a growing branch and ATM network and digital channels such as Netbanking, Phonebanking and MobileBanking.
